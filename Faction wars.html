Faction Wars

Faction Wars is a game mode in which you matches you up with other players' teams for a 3v3 battle.When players are new to the game, they will be prompted to choose one out of five factions:

  • Black Dragon
  • Brotherhood of shadow
  • Lin Kuei
  • Special Forces
  • White Lotus

Faction wars season

Faction wars season season resets every 4 days and rewards you different based of your season rank. To rank up you need Battle Rating which is gathered by playing Quick Play or Survivor Mode. The rewards you get are blood rubies, equipment cards and if your rank is Kombatant I or more you'll get a blood ruby pack.

Quick Play

In Quick Play you choose in between 4 difficulties:

Quick Battle
-Includes 1 fight
Normal Battle
-Includes 3 battles
Hard Battle
-Includes 5 battles
Fatal Battle
-Includes 7 battles

Faction wars store

The Faction Wars Store is a store where characters can buy exclusive characters and Epic Equipment for the cost of Blood Rubies. This is the only use for Blood Rubies. The Faction Wars Store can be accessed through the main Faction Wars page.
