Begginer guide

PvP

PvP is as important as progress. You'll need to know how to fight no matter where you've gone.
The first thing you need to know is basic PVP. How to attack and block. Basic attacks are easy as you just need to tap on your screen. After taping 3 times you'll recieve a minigame which leads to an "combo ender". In order to block you have to hold down with 2 fingers , but you might not do it fast enough.
Once again I'd recomend you watching another video from Mand in which you'll learn not only how to fight but and how to use characters properly.
